Our Story

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower travelers to explore the world with confidence and curiosity. We strive to create unforgettable experiences that foster cultural understanding, promote sustainable tourism practices, and enrich the lives of our clients and communities alike.

Our Vision

Our vision at Travels wild is to redefine the way people experience travel, setting new standards of excellence in the industry. We envision a world where every journey is an opportunity for discovery, where borders are bridges, and where the transformative power of travel transcends boundaries.

History

Travel wild was founded in 2024 by Kiel, a passionate traveler with a vision to share the joy of exploration with the world. What began as a small endeavor fueled by a love for adventure has since grown into a leading authority in the travel industry, renowned for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation.

Our Team

At Travels wild, our team is comprised of passionate travel enthusiasts who are dedicated to creating exceptional experiences for our clients. With diverse backgrounds, expertise, and a shared love for exploration, we work together seamlessly to turn your travel dreams into reality.

What we do

Visa Assistance

From compeleting application forms to scheduling embassy appointments, we handle every aspects of the process with efficiency.

Travel Insurance

Guidance on travel insurance options to provide coverage for unforeseen events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage.

Travel Assistance

Providing access to travel resources and information, including travel tips, packing guides, destination insights, and safety advisories.

Hotel Accomodations

Help in finding and booking suitable accommodations such as hotels, resorts, bed-and-breakfasts, or vacation rentals based on preferences, budget, and location.

Flight Reservations

Assisting clients in finding and booking flights, hotels,resorts, rental accomodations, or other lodging options that suit their needs and budget.

Transportation Services

Arrangements for ground transportation, including airport transfers, car rentals, train tickets, and guided tours.
